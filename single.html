YeonJun mixtape

On September 6, 2024, it was announced that Yeonjun will be making his solo debut later that same month with his first mixtape. On September 8, 2024, BigHit announced that he would be releasing a pre-release of "GGUM" on September 19, ahead of the release of his first mixtape "Yeonjun's Mixtape: GGUM".

Ggum

TXT’s Yeonjun is making a splash on the international music charts with his solo debut track!

September 21, BIGHIT MUSIC announced that Yeonjun’s new solo song “GGUM” had debuted at No. 1 on Oricon’s daily digital singles chart in Japan. Notably, Yeonjun was the only foreign artist to make the chart that day.

“GGUM” also reached No. 1 on Line Music’s realtime Top 100 Songs chart in Japan.

Additionally, as of 9 a.m. KST on September 20, “GGUM” had already hit No. 1 on iTunes Top Songs charts in at least 32 different regions—and the song also debuted at No. 1 on Spotify’s Top Songs charts in at least seven different regions, including South Korea, Thailand, and Saudi Arabia.

Meanwhile, as of midnight KST on September 21, Yeonjun’s music video for “GGUM” had reached No. 1 on YouTube’s Top Trending Videos in 31 different regions—and No. 1 on YouTube’s Top Trending Music Videos in 40 different regions.
